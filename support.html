Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open Yellow → tap Profile (bottom-right tab) → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete account. All your data is permanently removed from our servers.

How do I turn off notifications?

Profile → Notifications → toggle off the reminders you don't want. Or turn off all notifications for Yellow from your iPhone's Settings → Notifications → Yellow.

How do I disconnect Apple Health?

Open Apple's Health app → Profile → Apps and Services → Yellow → Turn Off All. Yellow never stores HealthKit data on our servers, so revoking access takes effect immediately.

I can't sign in.

Make sure you're using the same email address you signed up with. If you signed up with Apple or Google, use that button (not the email + password form). For password resets, tap Forgot password? on the sign-in screen.
